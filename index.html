    Welcome to the QBB project

    The Quantum Building Blocks (QBB) project aims at providing a powerful set of facilities to ease the development of state-of-the-art simulations. While the project has a slight focus on quantum mechanics, many of its libraries are nonetheless useful for all kinds of other projects.

    Currently, we are busily preparing our online presence and the first chunks of work, which will be released over the course of the next few month.
